List of Compositions for Easy Piano Trio

Note: these compositions have been selected mainly on the basis of their titles. Their technical level may vary considerably.

As regards the violin part, for a list of items in 1st pos see Easy Works for Violin category, and for 1st to 3rd pos, see Intermediate Works for Violin category.

For a complete guide to the repertoire, see pages 232-240 of Heim's Violin Repertoire Guide published in 1900. As a guide, Heim’s classification is as follows:
  • 1: 1st position
  • 2: 1st to 3rd
  • 3: 1st to 7th pos easy
  • 4: 1st to 7th pos difficult
  • 5: very difficult.
Note that Heim’s guide only goes up to the year 1900, and deals mainly with items published in Germany. However, to help the reader, Heim’s classification has been put in brackets as follows: (H1) means Heim level 1, and (H2) means Heim level 2. Items in Heim’s list at grade 3 to 5 have been omitted.

Schmidt of Heilbronn published a series of easy trios. See the list here.
